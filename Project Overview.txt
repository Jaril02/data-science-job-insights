Project Overview
This project focus on analyzing  real-world data-science job posting to extract and visualize current market trends such as in-demand skills, top hiring companies, job locations, and more.

Dataset Summary

- **Source**: [Kaggle - Data Science Job Salaries](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ruchi798/data-science-job-salaries)
- **File Name**: `data_science_jobs.csv`
- **Total Records**: 12217
- **Total Features (Columns): 15
- **Columns**:
  - 'job_link', 'last_processed_time', 'last_status', 'got_summary',
       'got_ner', 'is_being_worked', 'job_title', 'company', 'job_location',
       'first_seen', 'search_city', 'search_country', 'search_position',
       'job_level', 'job_type'
